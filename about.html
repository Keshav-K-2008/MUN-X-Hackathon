About EcoAction Canada

Connecting Canadians to a Sustainable Future

Our Story

EcoAction Canada was born from a simple observation: while Canadians care deeply about environmental sustainability, many struggle to understand how their individual actions contribute to national and global environmental goals. The disconnect between awareness and action needed a bridge.

Our project began as a grassroots initiative to empower everyday citizens with the tools, knowledge, and motivation to make meaningful environmental choices. We recognized that sustainability shouldn't be complicated or inaccessible‚Äîit should be as simple as checking your daily habits and seeing real-time impact.

Today, EcoAction Canada serves as a comprehensive platform that translates complex sustainability metrics into actionable, personalized insights for Canadians across all provinces and territories.

Aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals

Our platform directly supports Canada's commitment to the United Nations Development Programme's (UNDP) Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), adopted by all UN member states in 2015 as a universal call to action.

Ensuring sustainable consumption and production patterns through waste reduction and mindful choices.

SDG 13: Climate Action

Taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts through education and behavior change.

SDG 17: Partnerships for Goals

Strengthening implementation through community collaboration and shared sustainability objectives.
