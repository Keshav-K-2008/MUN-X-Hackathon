Frequently Asked Questions

How does EcoAction Canada work?

EcoAction Canada is a frontend-only platform that allows you to track your sustainable behaviors, view your environmental impact through dashboards, learn about UN SDGs, and participate in community challenges. All your data is stored locally in your browser, making the platform fast, private, and accessible.

Is EcoAction Canada free to use?

Yes! EcoAction Canada is completely free for all Canadians. Our mission is to make sustainability education and tracking accessible to everyone across the nation.

How is my Eco-Score calculated?

Your Eco-Score is calculated based on the sustainable actions you track across four categories: waste management, energy consumption, water conservation, and transportation. Each action has a point value reflecting its environmental impact. The more consistent and diverse your sustainable behaviors, the higher your score.

Can I participate in challenges from any province?

Absolutely! All challenges are open to Canadians from any province or territory. We also have province-specific leaderboards so you can see how your region is performing.

How can I suggest new features or challenges?

We love hearing from our community! Use the contact form above to share your ideas, or reach out to us via email or social media. Your feedback helps us improve and expand the platform for all Canadians.

Are the environmental statistics accurate?

Yes! All provincial data, SDG information, and environmental statistics are sourced from official government reports, Statistics Canada, Environment and Climate Change Canada, and UNDP resources. We regularly update our data to ensure accuracy.
